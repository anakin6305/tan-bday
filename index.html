Happy Birthday Tan ❤️

I don't even know where to start, because what we have isn't something that can be summed up easily. In just 2 years — and especially these last 10 months — you've become someone I can't imagine my life without.

You call me a kid, and honestly… I am a kid in front of you. But not because I'm immature — it's because I feel the safest with you. With you, there's no judgment, no ego, no pretending… just a pure, real bond. And that's rare. That's everything.

But don't get it twisted 😌 just because I'm your "kid" doesn't mean I don't take care of you. In my own way, I babysit you too — with all the care, affection, and love I have for you. It goes both ways, always.

From those random bus conversations and endless gossip, to studying together (or at least trying to), to exploring street food like we own the place… every little moment with you just hits different. And then our late-night talks — the deep ones, the chaotic ones, the ones that somehow mean the most — I cherish all of it more than I can explain.

You bully me, I ragebait you, and somehow in between all that chaos… we built something so strong, so real, and so irreplaceable.

Your presence alone makes me happy. You don't even have to do anything — just you being there is enough.

On your birthday, I just want you to know this — I'm always here. No matter what changes, no matter where life takes us, I hope this bond stays exactly like this… effortless, real, and forever ours.

Happy Birthday again, aunty… or should I say mom? 😌❤️ Stay the same, because that's exactly who I love the most.

— Your "kid" 🫶
